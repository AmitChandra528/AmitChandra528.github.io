Cinematographer

I’m a film student currently studying cinema (specialisation in cinematography) at the Shobhit University, which allows me to understand storytelling both in front of and behind the camera. My work is inspired by emotions, natural lighting, and cinematic composition—always exploring new ways to create impactful visuals.
